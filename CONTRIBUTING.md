# Contributing to Blink in the Bramble

Thank you for your interest in helping with the development of *Blink in the Bramble*! 

## How You Can Help
As per the license, this project is **Source-Available**. You are welcome to:
*   **Report Bugs:** Use the GitHub Issues tab to report any crashes or mechanical issues.
*   **Suggest Features:** Share your ideas for tactical stances or narrative moments.
*   **Submit Code/Fixes:** You may submit Pull Requests (PRs) directly to this repository.

## Contribution Rules
1.  **No Private Forks:** Please do not create public forks of this repository. Work on your changes locally and submit them via PR.
2.  **Assignment of Rights:** By submitting a PR, you agree to the terms in `LICENSE.md`, granting the project owner the right to use your code as part of the final game.
3.  **Tone & Vision:** Ensure your contributions align with the "emotionally grounded, dark fantasy" tone established in the GDD.

---
*Happy developing!*

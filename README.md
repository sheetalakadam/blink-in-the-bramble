# Blink in the Bramble

A dark fantasy JRPG set in Tessavar — a world shattered when the divine realm broke apart 300 years ago. While the gods experienced only a single day of crisis, three centuries of mortal life, culture, and struggle have flourished in the wound.

Now, as the gods look back and the angels begin to wake, **Zi**, a former soldier living in isolation, must navigate a world caught between divine "restoration" and its own hard-earned sovereignty.

## 🌿 Core Premise
*   **The Time Gap:** One day in the divine realm equals 300 years for mortals. The gods are operating on outdated information.
*   **The Wound:** The world grew in the cracks of a divine fracture. What the gods call "corruption," the mortals call "life."
*   **The Third Path:** Choose between restoring the old divine order, maintaining the fractured independence, or forging a new architecture for the world.

## ⚔️ Gameplay Systems
*   **Momentum Combat:** A shared party gauge that dictates the flow of battle. Shift between Grounded, Balanced, and Surge states to manage risk and reward.
*   **Stance Mastering:** Switch character stances as free actions to exploit enemy armor types (Crushing, Counter, Disruption, Knowledge, Break).
*   **Inner Monologue:** A multi-layered dialogue system where you see Zi’s unspoken thoughts alongside the spoken word.
*   **Domain Expertise:** Characters perceive the world through their professional lenses—tactical geometry, social economy, or cultural practice.

## 🛠 Technical Foundation
Built with **Godot 4**, utilizing:
*   **Seamless Chunking:** An asynchronous map-loading system for an open-world feel.
*   **Modern 2D Lighting:** High-fidelity lighting and post-processing (Bloom, Depth of Field) for an "Eastward-style" aesthetic.
*   **Resource-Driven Data:** Character stats, skills, and items are handled via Godot's native `.tres` Resource system for efficient iteration.
